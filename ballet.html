BALLET

Ballet is a style of dance that originated as a court dance during the Italian Renaissance in the fifteenth century and was later developed into a concert dance in France and Russia. It has since become a widespread, highly technical form of dance with its own vocabulary based on French terminology. It has been globally influential and has defined the foundational techniques used in many other dance genres and cultures. Ballet has been taught in various schools around the world, which have historically incorporated their own cultures. A ballet, consists of the choreography and music for a ballet production. The ballet that we know today was pioneered by Balanchine and is quite different from ballet when it first originated.
